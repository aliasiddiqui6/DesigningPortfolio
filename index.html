Translating Complex Science into Visual Clarity

Hi, I am Alia Siddiqui. I am a final-year Biomedical Science undergraduate blending high-throughput data analysis, space biology, structural modeling and 3D modeling with visual storytelling. Whether it is presenting multi-omics data for hepatocellular carcinoma or pitching astrobiology concepts, my goal is to make dense scientific research accessible, accurate, and visually compelling.
