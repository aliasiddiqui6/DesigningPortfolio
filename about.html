My Journey

Bridging the gap between complex data and clear communication.

The Intersection of Science and Design

As a final-year Biomedical Science undergraduate at Acharya Narendra Dev College, University of Delhi, my research focuses on high-throughput data analysis, multi-omics data integration, and space biology.

However, conducting research is only half the battle; communicating it effectively is just as critical. I started designing scientific posters, pitch decks, and 3D models to ensure that dense datasets—whether regarding hepatocellular carcinoma biomarkers or Martian terraforming—are accessible, accurate, and visually engaging.
